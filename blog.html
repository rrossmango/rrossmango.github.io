Words to Live By


Do without saying, don't say without doing. Underpromise, overdeliver. Don't mention you are working on something until you are confident it can be finished.

You will often feel scared of starting a new challenge, or worried you won't do it properly. Stare directly at the problem until you can move onto the next one.

The three best things you can be are honest, reliable, and decisive.
